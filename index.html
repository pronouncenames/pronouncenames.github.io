ABOUT THE SITE

Effective communication skills can lead to better lives and careers. Pronunciation is essential to communication in speaking. Many times, I was in the middle of a delightful conversion with my friends and I tried to mention some interesting things for their amusement. Suddenly, it went to astray because I couldn’t pronounce the things, like names of places, names of dishes, name of athletes, etc. Even worse, I pronounced these names wrong. Embarrassingly I had to repeat what I said; but my audience always looked at me with great doubts and uncertainty, struggling to figure out what I was talking about. The communication was interrupted and stopped. In order to avoid these embarrassing moments, I think I have to do something. This is the reason I create this PronounceEverything.com web site to collect lists of names with their PROUNOUNCES. I also create this site in memory of my English teacher Tom Bello.
