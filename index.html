ABOUT THE SITE

Effective communication skills can lead to better lives and careers. Correct pronunciation is an essential component in clear communication. Many times, I have been in the middle of a delightful conversion with friends and have tried to come up with interesting things to say to amuse them. Suddenly, it went astray because I could not pronounce certain words such as names of places, names of dishes, name of athletes, etc. Further, I pronounced these names incorrectly. Embarrassingly, I found myself having to repeat what I said; but my audience always looked at me with great doubt and uncertainty, struggling to figure out what I was trying to say. Communication was often interrupted. In order to avoid these humbling moments, I decided I had to do something. For this reason I created the PronounceEverything.com web site to collect lists of names with their pronunciations. I also created this site in memory of my English teacher, Tom Bello. He taught me the most important lesson of my life: everyone has a name; everything has a name; call them by their names. Here is an example of how to pronounce the State of

Virginia
